# Abstract # Sessions

Build a Question Answering System Overnight

@ ESWC 2018

With this tutorial, we aim to provide the participants with an overview of the field, insights into commonly faced problems, its recent trends and developments. At the end of the tutorial, the audience would have hands-on experience of developing two working QA systems: one based on rule-based semantic parsing, and another, a deep learning based method. In doing so, we hope to provide a suitable entry point for the people new to this field, and ease their process of making informed decisions while creating their QA solutions.
